About Me

I'm 26 years old, I'm a husband to Sandy Bramer and we've been married over 4 years now! I'm also a father, Sandy and I have one son, Everett and he is 3 years old!

I love Jesus and I'm a worship leader at our church, One Community Church! This year, I'll be preaching at our church 3 times - I'm really excited about this!
