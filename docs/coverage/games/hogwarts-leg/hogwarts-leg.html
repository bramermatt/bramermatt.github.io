Hogwarts Legacy: New Game

Hogwarts Legacy: New Game

Posted September 2, 2022

Coverage

New Game

I've recently been reading the Harry Potter books and that's gotten me really excited for the entire Harry Potter universe. So, when I heard about this new Harry Potter game I got very interested!

This game is being developed by Avalanche Software, the company that brought us Mad Max!

First off, the Avalanche Software website is really cool! It's all setup for Hogwarts Legacy! Second, I found it very difficult to find all the games that Avalanche makes, and that's what I was trying to do to get some information on the Mad Max game.

The original tweet came out September 16, 2020 and the we've only recently gotten the confirmation that the game with be released on February 10, 2023, so quite some time to be waiting on a game.

Hogwarts Legacy will launch on February 10, 2023 for PlayStation, Xbox, and PC. The Nintendo Switch launch date will be revealed soon. The team is excited for you to play, but we need a little more time to deliver the best possible game experience. pic.twitter.com/zh0EsOvDb7

— Hogwarts Legacy (@HogwartsLegacy) August 12, 2022

I've just got to say, I'm a little apprehensive based on some of the trailers, and also, the social media team @HogwartsLegacy are nearly bombarding us with content. That's sometimes not always a good sign. So, be weary of that. But, if you're excited please be excited!
