Live Updates on Ukraine-Russian War

Living in America, I don't know much about what's going on in Ukraine right now with the Russian invasion of Ukraine. And that is sad. I don't watch the news, I barely hear what's going on outside of the US. Maybe that's by design. But, today, I'm going to talk about the a game company that I stumbled on who is directly dealing with the Russian invasion of Ukraine.

Before we start let's get some live updates on the Russian invasion of Ukraine. Here are some pertinent links for you to follow to keep up on the war.

  • USIP Expert Analysis on Russia's War in Ukraine

When I First Heard of Frogwares

I saw a tweet from People Make Games' Chris Bratt in late August. I recently started following People Make Games, and I've been really impressed with their content. But I saw this tweet and got me interested in learning more about the Frogwares Team, their new game, and the Ukraine-Russian War.

At last week's Gamescom Congress, I interviewed two Ukrainian developers, Iryna & Nika of @Frogwares, about their experience creating games in a country under siege.

About Frogwares

I've very excited to shed the light on this company, and I believe it is import to shed light on the war as well.

This is directly from Frogwares website, about their company.

Created in the year 2000, Frogwares is an independent game development studio, based in Ukraine and Ireland. We are behind the Sherlock Holmes series and a Lovecraftian detective-horror game, The Sinking City. Thanks to the work of our talented team of artists and programmers, our games are available for all major platforms, including PlayStation, Xbox, PC and Nintendo Switch. Our latest title, Sherlock Holmes Chapter One, is now available on PC, PlayStation 5|4 and Xbox Series X|S.

Here is the link to the Frogwares Website! They have made a lot of games in their 22 years. The most recent game they are working on Sherlock Holmes The Awakened. This is a remake of the game that they made in 2007 link here!

Sherlock Holmes The Awakened

I'm going to be keeping my eye on this game, and you should too! The game is now on Kickstarter to be funded! It is also up on Steam to be added to your Wishlist. Frogwares spoke about their launch on Kickstarter here!

  • Kickstarter Campaign
  • Steam Page

This game has been very difficult to develop, obviously being under attack from Russia. The team at Frogwares was able to sit down and have an open discussion and complete a Question and Answer forum, find that here! This is a must read and watch.

Let's Pray for Ukraine

As I said, I don't know much about war or the current Ukraine-Russian war, but let's please pray for the country of Ukraine and the country of Russia. Unspeakable horrors have occurred and are occurring there everyday. May God bring peace and hope to the Ukrainians battling for the freedom and their lives.
