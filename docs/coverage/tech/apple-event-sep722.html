Event Preview

I'm trying to go into this event completely blind. I don't want to know anything about the event before I watch it for myself.

This has been my thoughts on these Apple Events for a few years now, and I'm not sure if that's just the stagnation of just how good phones are these days, or what, I'm just not sure.

The iPhone is a great phone, Samsung makes great phone, even Google makes great phones.

What is something that my phone (iPhone 12 Pro Max) can do that is better than your grandmother's iPhone 6? Okay, better battery life yes, better camera yes, but what else? Better screen yes. But, let's say you have an iPhone 12 Pro Max, do you need the new iPhone 14? Probably not.

Now this might get very existential very quickly, but hear me out.

Phones are great, and you don't have to upgrade them every year.

However, for me, I love new Apple Products. And, my phone is ready for an upgrade. Will I be getting the new iPhone 14 Pro Max when it comes out... probably. I'm excited to get a new color for my phone, how silly does that sound. My phone will be exactly as good as the new iPhone 14 Pro Max, but I'll still get a new iPhone because I love new gadgets.

What To Expect

So far as I can presume, we'll get a new iPhone, new Apple Watch, and possibly new iPads. What's new right?
