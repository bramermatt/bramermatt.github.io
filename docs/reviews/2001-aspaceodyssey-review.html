2001: A Space Odyssey Review

February 13, 2023

Movie Reviews

Written Review

This is the classic movie 2001: A Space Odyssey, and I did not enjoy it. I was bored and confused and this took me a few days to watch. That's okay, but I just didn't enjoy my time. I wanted to finish the movie and that's why I gave it the score I gave it.

The visuals were great here, but the story just took a long time to get into anything, and I wasn't impressed with the story.

Spoiler Alert

The Apes

THE DAWN OF MAN, when I saw this.. oh boy I was scared. Scared, not as in I didn't want to watch, scared, as in "what on earth amd I about to see?". And in fact, what on earth did I just see? This was crazy and clearly these were just people in Ape suits and I laughed more than anything. But then this object showed up, and okay?
