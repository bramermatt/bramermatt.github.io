    • Written Review

    Harry Potter, a now beloved franchise for myself. In my first Harry Potter Review: Harry Potter and The Sorcerer's Stone, I was very excited about reading Harry Potter. And Book One was 5/5 for me!

    Book Two, Chamber of Secrets, also a 5/5!

    I am enjoying the writting style of J.K. Rowling very much so. I appreciate the stories she told, and I really enjoyed the mystery with this book. I didn't know the answer to the mystery of the whole book, and that exciting!

    Harry and Ron have truly developed as characters and I'm enjoying Dumbledore as headmaster!

    Final Words

    There isn't much more to say, other than, if you've not read Harry Potter for yourself, I'd highly recommend!

    Next Book

    My next book is more than likely going to be Book Two of the Stormlight Archive: Words of Radience by Branden Sanderson!
