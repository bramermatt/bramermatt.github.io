    • Written Review

    First I think it's best for you to watch the trailer of Ted Lasso, please enjoy!

    Now that's out of the way, let's talk Ted Lasso: Season One!

    Season One

    I really enjoyed this show! I laughed, I cried, I loved it! The characters were awesome, there wasn't a single character that I didn't like! The humor was incredible, the story was exciting, the experience was great!

    From left to right: Nate, Coach Beard, Rebecca, Ted, Roy, Keely, and Leslie

    Apple TV+

    The Apple TV 4K

    As with everyone who has Apple TV+, I somehow have Apple TV+. I didn't buy it, I don't pay a subscription, I might have a free trial but I never signed up for one.

    Overall, I'd say the experience is pretty legendary! It works seamlessly with all my Apple Devices and syncs up pretty nicely when I transition from screen to screen.

    My Apple TV Widget

    Here you can see some shows I've been watching. For some reason it says I'm on Ted Lasso Season One Episode Nine, but I just started Season Two Episode One today, so I'm not really sure the mixup here.

    Final Score

    I give Ted Lasso Season One on Apple TV+ a 5/5 Excellent!
