    Wow! A thrill ride from start to finish. The movie made me laugh, cry, scream, and clap!

    I don’t have many words regarding Top Gun Maverick, other than “you’ve got to see it!”

    Original Top Gun

    Top Gun was a movie I watched a lot with my parents when I was younger. They loved the movie and I did too! It was a fun movie, and I enjoyed the story when I was younger.

    I rewatched the original Top Gun recently, and I have to say, it still holds up. Story wise, it’s great! However, the cinematic have absolutely nothing on Top Gun Maverick.

    You’ve got to watch this for yourself, just to see the aerial photography.

    This clip is from the movie, and absolutely doesn’t spoil anything. Please watch it!

    Top Gun Maverick

    This movie does not disappoint. The music is incredible, the story is fast-paced and enjoyable. There is a particular character who is somewhat integral who I didn’t remember from the Original Top Gun. That definitely does not deter from the movie in anyway!
