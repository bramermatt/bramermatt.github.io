Twilight Review

July 11, 2022


Written Review

So, not much to say here, truly. I love this book! 5/5 no doubt about it!

June 1 to July 11

As I'm writing this, I'm realizing that June 1 to July 11 is not very long to read a book, but my life has been hectic! I feel I should've read this in a much shorter length of time. However, I was too invested in this website or other tasks to read.

Audio Book vs Physical Book

This is a topic in and of itself, but I love a physical book. However, in shorter bursts I love an audio book! I can listen to a book while I cut grass or while I'm driving to work, or anywhere! I downloaded the audiobook from Audible when I had 200 pages left. I flipped on 2.0x read speed and I flew through these last 200 pages. And I loved it!

If I were able to read and listen at the same time, I'd sit down with my AirPods in and read and listen along and that was very enjoyable. That helped me get over the slump of reading after a few weeks break.
