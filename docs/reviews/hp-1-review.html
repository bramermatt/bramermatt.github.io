Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone Review

February 10, 2022

Video Review

Written Review

Oh my goodness! This has been the most enjoyable read THIS YEAR! Out of The Hobbit, Star Wars: Aftermath, Carrie, and the Silent Patient. I hope to reread this book every year.

This book felt like a cozy warm blanket, and reminded me of my childhood with my best friends. Quite an enjoyable read, a quick read at that. Only took me a week to finish!

I look forward to continuing the series!
