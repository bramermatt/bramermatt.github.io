Bike Commuting: Part Nine

August 1, 2022

Bike Tour - Leaving for Work

Today was my first offical Bike Commute to Work! At the time of writting this portion of my blog, I have yet to ride home. We'll see how that goes, and I'll add that to the end of the blog.

My bike!

Ride to Work

The Ride was a beautiful ride! I got to ride in with the sunrise, and it was amazing! The ride was tough for sure. I packed the bike with all sorts of things, as you saw in the video.

I had my backpack (heavy) and a pannier pack filled with body wash / shampoo, clothes, lunch, and 3 water bottles, and a coffee. HEAVY. Too heavy. It was tough because I hadn't ridden with any of those things on before, but I survived. I had to get off and walk a bit of the Walking Bridge because of the gears on my bike (my fault), but other than that a nice ride.

Shower Time

I made it work, congrats, now what? Time to shower, luckily there is a shower at my work. So I took advantage of it and it was delightful. Water started out cold, but warmed up and felt good to get cleaned up for a hard day's work!
