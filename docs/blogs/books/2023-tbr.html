Reading My TBR Books for 2023

January 8, 2023

I'm Having Such a Great Time Reading

2023, like 2022 looks like it's going to be an amazing year of reading! Here is a picture of my bookshelf, and as you can see there are a few different types of genres that I am going to be reading this year. As I wrote in the blog that said I have to read what's on my bookshelf before I buy anymore books, that's just what I'm going to do. These are all books that I purchased in 2022, because I wanted to read them, but I was reading something else. I'm currently reading The Dragon Reborn by Robert Jordan, book three in The Wheel of Time series, and I'm absolutely loving it! I received it as a Christmas gift from my parents - it was not on my bookshelf and I didn't purchase it for myself, so that still counts as being able to read it before I buy anymore books..

My Bookshelf, as of January 2023

2023 TBR

Starting from left to right, on the shelf - because I have the books in alphabetical order by author right now. I haven't decided what order I'm going to read these in, but for right now, they're just in alphabetical order.

  1. The Terminal List by Jack Carr
  2. The Hunger Games by Stephanie Collins
  3. Leviathan Wakes by James S.A. Corey
  4. The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes by Arthur Conan Doyle
  5. The Fault in Our Stars by John Green
  6. Attack on Titan 1 by CHAJIME ISAYAMA
  7. Percy Jackson the Lightning Thief by Rick Riordan
  8. Fifty Shades of Grey by EL James
  9. IT by Stephen King
  10. The Shining by Stephen King
  11. Doctor Sleep by Stephen King
  12. A Game of Thrones by George R.R. Martin
  13. Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Askaban by J.K. Rowling
  14. Mistborn: the Final Empire by Brandon Sanderson
  15. The Fellowship of the Rings by J.R. Tolkien
  16. The Martin by Andy Weir

    17. And on the second shelf..

  17. Star Wars: Brotherhood by Mike Chen

Genres

So on here are, some classics like The Hunger Games, The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes, The Fault in Our Stars, Fifty Shades of Grey, IT, The Shining, A Game of Thrones, and the Fellowship of the Ring. Others include some of my most anticipated reads, because I love the author or the series - and that's Mistborn: The Final Empire by Brandon Sanderson and Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Askaban by J.K. Rowling. I really look forward to reading those, but I don't konw when I'll get to those with all these books that are on my list.

Read All These in 2023?

I don't actually think that I'll have read all these books by the end of 2023, but it's a list at least. I know that I'll be reading these books and these alone - so that at least makes me happy to know that there is a plan in place. Now, it's just to decide which of these to read next!
