2023 is the Year of "In Review"

I love a great many things, and that is why you've seen so many different things with In Reviews. As of January 2023, I have Cosmere, Harry Potter, The Last of Us, The Lord of the Rings, Star Wars, Wheel of Time in Reviews. That's a lot, and that list will get longer and longer by the end of 2023! So, my thought is making a whole page dedicated to all the In Reviews. This will be like the Reviews page where there are separate sections for each franchise.

Video

Expect these reviews in 2023! - Video on Youtube

2023 In Reviews to Expect

Here starts the listing of all the NEW In Reviews I'd like to do this year!

MCU

With the NEW Ant Man movie coming out this year, I look forward to completing and entire backlog of Marvel Cinematic Universe or MCU in Review!

That's right, all 26+ Movies and TV Shows, all reviewed by me!

Ant Man and the Wasp Quantumania

Brandon Sanderson

I've got one more book, currently, on my shelf that is Brandon Sanderson and that is Mistborn: The Final Empire! That will be reviewed, and I hope to read more Brandon Sanderson's Cosmere this year too!

I may even pick up one of his new Secret Novels!

Brandon Sanderson

Stephen King

The Stephen King... I currently have IT, The Shining, and Doctor Sleep on my bookshelf to read - and those'll be reviewed for sure!

Stephen King

The Fast and the Furious

The Fast and the Furious, right now has nine movies, but there is a tenth coming out this year! I haven't seen number nine yet, so I think this'll be a great time to rewatch them all!

I also love the tagline for Fast X... "The end of the road begins". Meaning we could get so many more Fast movies!

Fast X Poster

Star Wars

The Star Wars Universe has a lot of movies left to review, TV Shows to Review, and many more books to review! Going on the list!

Star Wars Logo

The Last of Us

The Last of Us has two games, and a few more episodes of Season One left! Tons to review!

Last of Us on HBO

The Wheel of Time

The Wheel of Time is a 15 book series, and I've only read 3 so far. That's a lot left to review, AND there is a TV Show that I haven't reviewed yet! ON THE LIST IT GOES!

The Eye of the World, Book One

Expectations

In total for 2023 you should expect... and wow; I'm so excited!
