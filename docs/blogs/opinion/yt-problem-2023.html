YouTube Shorts.. I have a Problem

January 20, 2023

Blogs

My Problem

I've been having a problem with YouTube.com ever since YouTube Shorts became a reality..

YouTube Shorts have impeded on my YouTube subscriptions, when I feel that should not happen. When I go to select Subscriptions - I'd expect to see videos from my subscriptions, but I see videos and YouTube Shorts. Which makes sense, but I'd really like to be able to separate the Shorts from the Videos.. but I cannot.

Screenshot of YouTube Subscriptions Tab

Subscriptions Tab

I'd love to be able to browse the Subscriptions tab.. and see I also have the same problem on mobile! I can't escape YouTube Shorts.

YouTube Home

I've been doing a lot of my YouTube browsing exclusively within the Home tab.. as seen below. I like this tab because I get to see recommended videos and also my subscriptions. And, as you can see from the screenshot.. Shorts are separated! That's what I like to see.

Screenshot of YouTube Home

YouTube Shorts

Now don't get me wrong, I love watching YouTube Shorts, when I want to watch them.. they're great! Really, more often than not, just a remix of a TikTok, but still nice.

Screenshot of YouTube Shorts Tab

Please YouTube

Please YouTube, give me a separator tab for Shorts and Subscriptions.
