    • Why is this a topic?

    My interests change, nowadays at least, like the weather in the Ohio Valley. One day high of 93, the next high of 63. That's exactly like my interests. One day it's learning Hebrew so that I can read my Bible in it's original language, the next it's Lego Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga on my Nintendo Switch.

    So that's why I made this topic, because my intersts change and that's okay! That's what make me.. me!

    July 2022

    And these are in no particular order, and they never will be. Just as I write, this is the order I choose.

    • Lego Star Wars: The Skywalker Saga on the Nintendo Switch

    • Reading Books, Mostly Fantasy Genre

    • News
    • Watching my Son Grow Up
