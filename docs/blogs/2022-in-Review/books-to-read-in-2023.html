How Many Books To Read in 2023

How Many Can I Read?

My goal for 2022 was to read 8 books, and I read that in 7 months. And I totalled the year, if you read my How Many Books Did I Read in 2022 blog, you'll have seen that I read 16 books (so far - it's December 12) so there is still time!

For 2023...

For 2023, I am going to try to read at least 16 Books! So that is the same amount as this year, but I was only expecting to read 8 books and I ended up reading 16, so let's try for that again! I imagine, if I complete that goal for next year, I may increase my 2024 Reading Goal, but we shall see when that time comes!

16 Books, I Think I Can Do That

We'll just have to see!
